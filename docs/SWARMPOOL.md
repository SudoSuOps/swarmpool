# Swarmpool

Swarmpool is the **visible field of opportunity**.

Jobs are published openly so compute providers can decide when and how to participate.

---

## Properties

- IPFS-native
- Content-addressed
- Signed snapshots
- No inbound APIs

---

## What Lives in Swarmpool

- JOB snapshots
- CLAIM snapshots (optional)
- PROOF submissions (raw)

---

## Canon Rule

> Swarmpool declares opportunity. It does not declare truth.

---

Swarm provides verifiable inference infrastructure.
Models, data, and outcomes remain the responsibility of their operators.
